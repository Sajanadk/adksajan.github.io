About University
More about us

Ocean International University of Creativity is a pioneering academic institution nestled in the heart of Sanothimi Bhaktapur, Nepal. Since its establishment on Magh 1st, 2040 BS, the university has been a symbol of innovation and excellence in the Nepalese educational landscape.

The university is dedicated to cultivating a culture of creative learning and exploration, and offers a wide range of programs in diverse fields such as fashion design, animal science, plant science, architecture, and many more.

At Ocean International University of Creativity, students are encouraged to think outside the box and push the boundaries of their own creativity. With state-of-the-art facilities, top-notch faculty, and a dynamic learning environment, the university provides an unparalleled educational experience that prepares students for success in today's ever-changing world.

Join us on our journey to unlock your full potential and become a trailblazer in your chosen field.
